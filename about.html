Career aspirations

In the short term, I want to master front-end development well enough to build polished, responsive interfaces without reaching for a tutorial every five minutes. From there I plan to learn back-end technologies and databases so that I can call myself a genuine full-stack developer by the time I graduate.

Looking further ahead, I would love to work on products that improve access to education and everyday services across Africa. Software built locally, for local problems, is where I believe I can make the biggest difference.
